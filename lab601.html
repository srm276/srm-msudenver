The Tarot

Origins

In English speaking countries, these cards are almost exclusively associated with fortune-telling, however, the Tarot were orginally playing cards from 16th century Italy. A traditional deck conttains a total of 78 cards, including 56 unamed cards divided into four suites; swords, cups, coins, and wands. This part of deck is comparable to the 52-standard French/Spanish/Italian-suited playing cards.

Major Arcana

In a regular card game (ex. Tarocchini), the 22 named cards correspond to trump cards in gaming. These named cards are sometimes referrred to as the Major Arcana, although the term Major Arcana is more closely connected with divination or fortune-telling. The Major Arcana is unique to Tarot, there is no equivalent in a standard 52-card deck.
