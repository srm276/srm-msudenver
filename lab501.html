The Tarot

More frequently associated with fortune-telling, the Tarot were orginally playing cards from 16th century Italy. A traditional deck conttains a total of 78 cards, 56 unamed cards divided into four suites; swords, cups, coins, and wands. This part of deck is comparable to the 52-standard French-suited playing cards.

Major Arcana

In English speaking countries, Tarot is best known for the Major Arcana. In a normal card game (called Tarocchini), these named cards act as the trump cards. The term Major Arcana is associated with divination. The Major Arcana is unique to Tarot, there is no equivalent in a standard 52-card deck.
